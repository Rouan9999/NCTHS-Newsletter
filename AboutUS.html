Our story

Welcome to Falcon Insiders, where innovation meets information! Founded by Emma Thompson, Alex Rodriguez, and Jordan Patel, our club is dedicated to injecting a dose of excitement into New Century Technology High School's news scene.
Thanks to the incredible help the Web Development club under the leadership of Rouan Nandjou, Josiah Mitchell and Ryan Towry we now have a sleek website to call home.
The Falcon Insiders are here to deliver real-time news with a tech-savvy twist, bringing you the latest stories from our vibrant school community.
Join us on this journey as we redefine school journalism and showcase the talents of NCTHS students.
